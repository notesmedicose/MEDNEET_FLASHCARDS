Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

Welcome to MEDNEET (referred to as "we", "our", or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application and related web services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a personalized learning experience and save your flashcard study progress, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Authentication Information: If you choose to log in using Email/Password or Google Sign-In (Gmail), we receive your email address, display name, and profile photo URL via Firebase Authentication.
  • Study Statistics: We track your flashcard review progress, SM-2 spaced repetition schedules, level progression, and study streak count. This data is stored locally on your device using secure SQLite storage.
  • Anonymous Device Data: We may collect generic anonymous device diagnostics to troubleshoot crashes and improve app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide user account synchronization and authenticated access to decks.
  • Implement the SM-2 algorithm to customize flashcard review intervals.
  • Display progress metrics, streaks, and user level progression inside the app dashboard.
  • Monitor and fix application bugs and stability issues.

3. Data Sharing & Third-Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. All data is stored securely using Google Firebase services (for authentication) and on-device SQLite storage. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising SDKs.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your profile data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and associated study data, you can do so by logging out and emailing our support team at support@neetprep.mednotes. Your authentication profile and credentials will be removed within 30 days of the request.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard encryption protocols (HTTPS/SSL) for all communication between the app and Firebase Authentication servers. Local database files on your mobile device are secured using standard system permissions.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date above.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
